## 💡 Mr. GTM Integration Insights:
* **The API Rule:** If you're building an AI Voice bot, check for **Webhooks**. Without them, you're just polling data and burning your rate limits.
* **Why the Matrix?** Don't buy a VoIP for the dialer. Buy it for the **Auto-Summarization**. If notes don't hit the CRM instantly, your reps are wasting 20% of their day.
* **The Winner:** The one that integrates with your stack in **one click**. Friction is the enemy of GTM scaling.
